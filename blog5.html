Alternatives to College

College has, for quite some time now, been proposed as the desired route for a young person to take before entering the professional world. However, this approach is not without its problems, such as the finite number of graduate jobs waiting for people fresh out of school, as well as the positions that are going unfilled because people aren’t looking at other career paths. There may be alternatives to college that prepare you for the workforce and a career that doesn’t require a college degree.
